Interested in becoming a Principal of FEW?

Becoming a principal is a commitment to FEW’s mission, and a larger opportunity to bring people together from all walks of life to discuss issues surrounding gender equality and entrepreneurship. We ask, on a yearly basis, that principals organize one event, contribute an original thought piece to our blog, and share relevant news and updates with the world through your own social networks.

If you’re interested in becoming a principal for F-E-W, please email us. We look forward to hearing from you!
