January 27, 2014 (6—8PM)
San Francisco, CA

Women in Tech: Fireside Chat with Freada Kapor Klein and Heather Hiles & Moderated by Vivek Wadhwa

It's no secret that being a female founder comes with a unique set of challenges. While fundraising can be difficult for all founders, recent studies show an alarmingly higher rate or fundraising success for male founders. Why is that? Is it a confidence issue? Is it unconscious bias telling investors that women are less capable leaders? Are women less likely to sell the hyper-growth storyline that investors crave? This event was organized by General Assembly and sponsored by FEW.
